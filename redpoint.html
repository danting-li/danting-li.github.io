WHAT IS REDPOINT WEBSITE USED FOR?
RedPoint is a luxury vacation Airbnb nestled near Joshua Tree Park, California. Recognizing the importance of showcasing their unique offerings and brand identity in a visually compelling manner, the Founder of RedPoint sought to create a brand logo and website that not only highlights their services but also conveys a harmonious identity, emphasizing their role as more than just a place to stay, but an integral part of the traveling adventure and wellness exploration. I led a team of an illustrator and a developer, providing a comprehensive range of services that included web and mobile design, web development, logo creation, and engaging copywriting.
UNDERSTAND THE BRAND
RedPoint is a cozy nest immersed in nature adventure. In an era marked by a growing emphasis on self-care, encompassing both physical and mental wellness, there's a burgeoning demand for urban escapes and wellness-focused travel experiences. RedPoint emerges as a serene sanctuary, offering travelers a welcoming haven away from the bustle of city life. Guests not only find comfort in their personal spaces but also discover a shared celestial backyard, ideal for gatherings with family and friends, all against the breathtaking backdrop of the desert vista.
Every aspect of RedPoint, from its design to its services, embodies the brand's spirit - a blend of adventure and self discovery of peace, inviting travelers to explore both the beauty of the natural world and the depths of their inner selves. With affordable luxury at its core, RedPoint promises a truly rejuvenating experience.

FINAL LOGO
The logo, depicting a figure carrying a sleeping pad amidst two mountains, embodies the shared experiences of guests immersed in nature's embrace, fostering peaceful connections with their surroundings.
Website
To capture the essence of RedPoint's personality, I drew inspiration from the nostalgic concept of a childhood treehouse-a sanctuary where one feels safe to stow away treasures, memories, and escape the daily grind, finding solace amid the chaos of the city.
With a focus on providing a genuine sense of urban escape and inner peace, I opted for a simple yet cozy layout infused with charming sketches and illustrations.
The final design aimed to evoke the nostalgic charm of flipping through the pages of a cherished picture book, beckoning guests on a journey of tranquility, and preparing them for enchanting adventures amidst nature's embrace.
